About

The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size. It is characterised by a gentle and affectionate nature and a striking golden coat. It is commonly kept as a pet and is among the most frequently registered breeds in several Western European countries.

Wikipedia Temperament: Friendly, Intelligent, Kind, Reliable, Trustworthy, Confident
Life expectancy: 10 – 12 years
Mass: 29 – 34 kg (Male), 25 – 29 kg (Female)
